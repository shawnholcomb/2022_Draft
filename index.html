Dates & General Info

The dates for the draft weekend this year are Friday, August 26th - Sunday, August 28th. The actual draft will be held Saturday afternoon/evening as usual.

The cabins we are looking at are 2-3 hours from DFW and current transportation plans involve a couple of us driving separate vehicles. Car rental prices have skyrocketed and a passenger van would cost ~ $860. The cost doesn't seem worth it for a short trip. That being said, we are hoping to meet locally around 12pm on Friday. When we determine which cabin we are going to rent we will determine that location. From there we will depart as a group, make stops for food and drinks (I heard Rosen is bringing the chips) and arrive at the cabin together. From there, shenanigans will commence. We plan on doing group meals for Friday dinner, Saturday lunch and Saturday dinner and we can figure those plans in the days to come.

Dope Pool Cabin

Price

$122.61 per person

Notes

Southeast of Dallas and as the title indicates, this "cabin" has a dope pool with a cool view. The outside area seems like it would be a fun place to lounge. Inside there is a tiki bar so the bartenders of the league can compete in a mojito making contest. Overall this house is pretty nice, though plain without too many extra-curricular activities to focus on (which could be an issue). The biggest concern with this place is that it is pretty close to a neighbor. They share a driveway so we are talking maybe 50 yards. The contract on this one has a specific warning about noise, which means they have likely dealt with this before, and it claims that they will evict us if they get noise complaints. No matter how much we try to keep the noise down, you guys know how it gets after everyone is half a case of White Claws deep. So that is the major concern here.

Approximately 2 hours from Dallas

El Barrio Cabin

Price

$174.51 per person

Notes

This spot is located in Mexia, TX, which means it is less in the woods and more in the coutry surrounding a ghetto town. There is a hookah lounge about 9 miles away though, so there's that! This spot has plenty of space inside for sleeping and kicking it and the outdoor space is pretty nice. There is a sketch pool but a hot tub, fire pit and some other amenities that are spread out across the back/side yard. As eluded to before, the drawbacks on this place are the location, which is basically a low-rent area and the pool is located across the street from a neighbor. The sleeping options are also the weakest at this spot.

Dave & Buster's Cabin

Price

$210.61 per person

Notes

This place is the first true cabin in the woods. It is located near Broken Bow, OK but is not in a community and truly isolated. It is about 20 minutes from the Broken Bow Choctaw Casino but there is nothing around it. We could truly get as loud as we want. Plenty of sleeping here but the biggest draw is the ground level in this cabin. There is a long bar setup as well as hightops and beanbags with a pool table, air hockey, ping pong, darts and horseshoes. If you get bored here, that's on you baby! It's also on a creek with a firepit and hot tub. The biggest downside from this is that it is slightly further away than some options and there is wifi but it is sketchy for video streaming so remote drafting could be a challenge. There are also reviews that state they did not get cell service. Overall though, this one does appear to check the boxes of being remote and having plenty of activities for the group.

Fancy Wedding Cabin

Price

$256.92 per person

Notes

Okay, bear with us on this one. We spent 20 minutes looking at this venue and thought it was pretty dope before we came across the big catch but we thought we would through it out here anyways. This spot is a wedding venue that also rents out for reunions and other events. The place is nearly brand new with great provision and a private pond. Plenty of space with a nice outdoor kitchen and sitting area. The catch? There is no indoor kitchen/living area! This means that most of our time would be spent outdoors. In August. In Oklahoma. The outdoor area has a gang of fans and there is a lot of covered area. The only real indoor lounge space is a bedroom area upstairs with a small couch, a handful of beds and a shuffleboard table. It's definitely unconventional but some people may dig it. The pics would probably be good for the gram, yo!

Flight Info

For those flying into Dallas for the draft, your two options for airport are Dallas Fort Worth International Airport (DFW) and Dallas Love Field Airport (DAL). Both options are viable with the biggest difference being that DAL is the less busy option and is easier to navigate (read smaller airport, but not in a bad way) than DFW. Our recommendation is to choose a flight that works best for you as far as price/options go (I know you doctors are gonna be in the sweet, sweet business class) and don't focus too much on the actual airport.

Another thing to consider is the time the flight arrives. The plan is to depart Dallas at 12pm so it would be great if flights arrived by 11am. This would give time to navigate the airport and get to the meeting space. If you have the capability and prefer to fly in the Thursday then let us know. We might be able to help out with sleeping arrangements if you want to crash on a couch and we can meet up Thursday night as well!

The last consideration is the departure time of your flight. Remember that returning to DFW will be a 2-3 hour drive. Optimally your flight wouldn't depart before 3pm, but we understand we do not live in a perfect world. If you run into issues and we need to leave the cabin earlier we can make arrangements. We don't want to stress anyone out with this decision so just speak with the Commish and we can work with the schedules.
